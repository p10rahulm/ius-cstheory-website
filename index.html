Mission Statement
Theoretical Computer Science research has produced and benefited from several powerful paradigms which bridge the discrete and continuous worldsâ€”for instance, convex relaxations of combinatorial optimization problems, spectral graph theory, and Boolean Fourier analysis. Polynomials are one such domain which hold promise for upcoming research work in theoretical computer science.
The center will facilitate research on the new and exciting topic of Polynomials as an Algorithmic Paradigm. The virtual center will support collaboration between the faculty and students from the involved universities as well as workshops and online seminars.
The center aims to foster research collaborations between partner research groups in India and the US, working in areas related to Optimization, Linear Programming, High Dimensional Probability and their applications to algorithmic domains.
Our Center is generously sponsored by a grant under the aegis of the India-US Science and Technology Forum (IUSSTF)
